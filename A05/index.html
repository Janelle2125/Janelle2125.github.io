Siargao Island in Surigao del Norte, Philippines, is a stunning destination known for its white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, coconut palm trees, and peaceful mangrove swamps.

Sugba Lagoon on Siargao Island offers turquoise waters, limestone cliffs, and lush trees for swimming, kayaking, and stand-up paddleboarding, a must-see destination for natural beauty.

Daku Island, a popular destination on island-hopping cruises in Siargao, is known for its white beaches, shimmering waves, and lush coconut palms, making it a popular destination for relaxation.
