Inside Out 2 – Exploring Our Emotions

Joy

Joy is the bright, enthusiastic leader of the emotions. She’s always optimistic and works hard to keep Riley happy, even when things get tough.

Sadness

Thoughtful and empathetic, Sadness helps Riley cope with difficult times. She brings emotional depth and connection.

Fear

Fear keeps Riley safe by anticipating danger and helping her avoid risky situations. He's always prepared for the worst.

Anxiety

Anxiety is fast-talking and intense. She’s new in *Inside Out 2* and represents Riley’s concern about the future and self-pressure.

Envy

Envy is small but expressive. She wants what others have, representing Riley’s comparisons and desire to fit in.

Ennui

Ennui reflects teen boredom and disinterest. Her sarcastic tone and lazy attitude bring a unique energy to Riley’s mind.

Disgust

Stylish and blunt, Disgust protects Riley from social embarrassment and bad experiences. She’s the guardian of good taste.
